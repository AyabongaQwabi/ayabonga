**2/10**  
**DevConf – The undisputed champ for professional SA software devs**

- Joburg: 12 May 2026 @ The Canvas @ Riversands
- Cape Town: 14 May 2026 @ Century City Conference Centre  
  One-day format (no multi-day zombie mode), 5 tracks, 35+ speakers.  
  Vibe: Enterprise-real. Expect deep dives into CI/CD pipelines, observability in prod, cloud architecture (AWS/Azure/GCP war stories), TypeScript patterns, Next.js in the wild, and yes — talks on building resilient apps when load shedding hits stage 6.  
  Hallway track = pure gold: braai-level chats with hiring leads from local fintech/e-comm giants.  
  Pros: SA-context heavy (our problems, our stacks), community-driven, affordable.  
  Cons: Sells out quick — already >50% gone.  
  Verdict: Non-negotiable. If you're in Gauteng, do Joburg. CPT folks, grab the home game. Show up with your Vercel link ready.  
  devconf.co.za

**3/10**  
**Africa Tech Festival (with The AI Summit Cape Town baked in)**

- 17–19 Nov 2026 @ CTICC, Cape Town  
  3-day beast covering telecoms, cloud, fintech, startups + dedicated AI stage.  
  Vibe: Big-ecosystem energy. You'll rub shoulders with investors, product leads, policymakers. Perfect for mid/senior devs eyeing AI-augmented workflows (Cursor/Claude/Grok in enterprise), cloud-native architecture, or pivoting into AI infra.  
  Pros: Massive networking (cross-pollinate with non-dev roles), AI Summit track will have 2026 gold on agentic coding, prompt engineering for SA use cases, edge AI during outages.  
  Cons: Broader than pure dev (less hands-on code, more strategy), tickets pricier, corporate crowd.  
  Verdict: If you're Joburg-based, worth the flight to CPT for the November push — pair it with a weekend braai. Great for career acceleration when the market cools.  
  africatechfestival.com

**4/10**  
**DataConf – The underrated one to watch**

- Aiming for Aug / early Sep 2026, Cape Town (venue TBC)  
  Community-driven, focused on data + AI (think Supabase/Postgres heavy, real-time pipelines, ML integration with modern JS stacks).  
  Vibe: Smaller, tighter-knit. Co-run by DevConf folks, so expect solid production-grade talks on RLS, caching strategies, observability for data flows — all with SA realities in mind.  
  Pros: Deep relevance if you're full-stack building dashboards/SaaS with real data. Less overwhelming than the big ones.  
  Cons: Dates/venue still soft — keep eyes on dataconf.co.za for CFP (mid-April/May expected).  
  Verdict: Bookmark + set reminder. If you're grinding Supabase + AI data apps, this could be your hidden gem.

**5/10**  
**TECHSPO Cape Town**

- 1–2 Oct 2026 @ V&A Waterfront Cape Town Avenue Conference Venue  
  2-day expo: Internet/Mobile/AdTech/MarTech/SaaS focus.  
  Vibe: More trade-show energy — booths, demos, startup pitches, sponsor swag. Good for seeing latest tools (AI IDEs, cloud services, payment gateways like Yoco/Ozow live).  
  Pros: Easy networking with vendors/tech providers, free expo pass options sometimes. Great if you're in CPT or building consumer-facing apps.  
  Cons: Lighter on deep technical talks — more marketing/innovation than code.  
  Verdict: Solid side quest if you're already in Cape Town or want leads/partnerships. Skip if you crave engineering depth.  
  techspocapetown.co.za

**6/10**

**7/10**  
Real talk from someone who's done most of these:

- Bring business cards (old school works) + your GitHub/Vercel/Supabase project QR code.
- Prep a 20-sec pitch: "Hey, I'm grinding Next.js + AI vibe coding in Joburg — built a stokvel tracker that survives loadshedding. Thoughts?"
- Network intentionally: target 5–10 meaningful chats, not spray-and-pray.
- Follow up on LinkedIn/X within 48h — that's where the magic happens.
